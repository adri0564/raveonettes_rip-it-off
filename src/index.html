Attack of the Ghost Riders

MØ

“I've been a massive fan of The Raveonettes since I was a teenager. Their lyrical, musical and visual universe has always been an inspiration to me - then and now. So it was an honour to be asked to do a cover for their "Whip It On" Ep anniversary, and I had so much fun doing "Attack Of The Ghost Riders", which is one of my all time favourite tracks of theirs.” - MØ

Veronica Fever

Brimheim

“I'm really happy with the organic interpretation of Veronica Fever. It stands in sharp contrast to the original's noisy coolness. We made it with an acoustic guitar, some brass and a dragging drumbeat that I found quite original myself. The Raveonettes were some of my idols growing up and "Whip It On" was the soundtrack to my first ever falling in love. I'm very proud that Sharin and Sune invited me to take part in this project. Feels full-circle to me.” - Brimheim

Do You Believe Her

Anton Newcombe

“I really enjoyed having the chance to play around with one of The Raveonettes' songs and I hope to see them play again soon.” - Anton Newcombe
