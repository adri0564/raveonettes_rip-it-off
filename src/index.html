Attack of the Ghost Riders

Mø

“I've been a massive fan of The Raveonettes since I was a teenager. Their lyrical, musical and visual universe has always been an inspiration to me - then and now … I had so much fun doing ‘Attack Of The Ghost Riders’.” - MØ

Veronica Fever

Brimheim

“I'm really happy with our organic interpretation of Veronica Fever. It stands in sharp contrast to the original's noisy coolness. We made it with an acoustic guitar, some brass and a dragging drumbeat that I found quite original myself. The Raveonettes were some of my idols growing up and ‘Whip It On’ was the soundtrack to my first ever falling in love … Feels full-circle to me.” - BRIMHEIM

Cops on Our Tail

“… when they asked me to do a cover of one of the songs from Whip It On , an album I’ve listened to a million times, the first thing that struck me was plain fear … I couldn’t use that for anything, so I went to my studio, drank some beers and did the cover version of Cops on Our Tail in one night." - Trentemøller

“We wanted to preserve that Raveonettes sound and do the song justice! They are incredible. We wanted it krauty and odd for their fans and ours. We love them and it was an honor to be asked to do this. We literally dropped everything at the chance." - The Black Angels

Bowels of the Beast

Prisma

“When we started working on Bowels of the Beast, our approach was to be as playful as possible. The song's already written, and it's really good, so our job was just to add a few more spices and put together its elements in an original way. It's been a super fun and inspiring process." - PRISMA
